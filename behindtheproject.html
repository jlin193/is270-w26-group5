Behind the Project:

Why a dossier?

This project was created by four graduate students at UCLA in the Department of Library and Information studies, for the course IS 270: Systems and Infrastructure during Winter 2026.

In the course, we’d been tasked to analyze the technological landscape of a contemporary Library and Information Science (LIS) role. Our final deliverable is a website, meant to serve as a knowledge artifact to support our fellow LIS practitioners in navigating our professional practice.


Tools Used:

  • Discord for team member communication

  • Google Documents for collaborative writing and editing, and taking meeting minutes

  • Google Drive for file management and sharing

  • Zoom to conduct and transcribe the interview
